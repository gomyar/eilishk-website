COLOUR/ART THERAPY COURSE

INNER PERSONALITIES - OUTER COMMUNITIES

Art is a powerful way of entering into the stream of images and colour in our unconscious and in our dreams.

In using art materials we switch off our brains and allow our hands to intuitively lead us to our core issues and to help resolve what we find there.

This course is intended to be a very active approach to your problems. Each course commences and finishes with choosing colour.

All materials are included.

No experience is necessary.
