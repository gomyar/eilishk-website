Inner Personalities, Outer Communities is my colour therapy work. With a background in community arts, which included drama and the use of art materials, I early came to the conclusion that everyone is creative, regardless of education or social status. But our "Inner Personalities" (you are too poor, thick, socially inferior etc) prevent us from reaching our full potential. People have to overcome these problems at a group level first but eventually as an individual and ultimately use their creativity to contribute to the good of all - hence the "Outer Communities".

When the time was right Aura-Soma Colour Therapy turned up in my life and I used it to encompass exploring these issues in association with dreams, art and personality type as well as colour in its own right.
