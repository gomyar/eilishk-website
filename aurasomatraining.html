Note: It is not neccessary to become a practitioner of Aura-Soma. All the levels of the training are part of a personal journey and exploration of the meaning of colour. They can be undertaken without any study requirements However to become a registered practitioner all 3 levels must be completed.

These courses are certified by ASIACT in the U.K, which is an educational trust dedicated to colour and research in colour located in Lincolnshire. It runs a worldwide network of training services to teachers and practitioners of Aura-Soma and contributes to international seminars on colour. It manages a 500 acre bio-dynamic farm in the UK. It also has both an extensive in-house programme of courses related to Aura-Soma and conducts a similar visiting programme internationally. There are annual trips to sacred sites (usually places like Easter Island, Glastonbury, Ireland etc).

Aura-Soma Ireland has a national distributor, a network of practitioners & teachers, a website (www.aura-soma-ireland.com), Facebook page (The Aura-Soma Colour-Care System in Ireland), regular training and colour courses and practice opportunities.

LEVEL 1, THE FOUNDATION COURSE

is a stand alone course and is useful:

  • For anyone interested in getting a basic introduction to colour
  • For the opportunity to experience colour directly and track it's effects on your own process.
  • For the opportunity to relate colour and Aura-Soma to your current practice, what ever that might be, and see how it might fit in and enhance your practice
  • For the first part of the 3 level training process for those interested in becoming registered practitioners.

The Course Covers:

The origins of Aura-Soma

Colour Theory

Colour and the Chakras/Subtle Anatomy

Experiencing & Interpreting the colour selections

Opportunities to practice with the colour selections under supervision

Meaning and use of Pomanders, Quintessences, Essences, Archangeloi

Colour sets in Aura-Soma (chakra, rescue, angel etc)

Introduction to The Masters and Aura-Soma

Practical uses for colour in your work can be examined (eg bio-energy, reflexology, reiki, etc)

I provide follow up practice sessions in Dublin and Countrywide.

BECOMING A PRACTITIONER

Practitioner training in the Aura-Soma Colour-Care System is in 3 parts

Level 1 (The Foundation, Blue Level) is a basic introduction to colour generally and Aura-Soma specifically.

Level 2 (The Intermediate, Yellow Level), builds on the first at a more "feeling" level

Level 3 (Advanced, Red Level), grounds what has gone before.
