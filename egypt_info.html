Egypt: Additional Information & Itinerary

The trip is available for 1 or 2 weeks.

Week 1, the Foundation Course, begins at Luxor. From there we will travel down/up the Nile by 5 Star air-conditioned cruiser, all accommodation, meals/trips to sites (see Itinerary below) included.

Week 2, the Intermediate Course, includes time in Luxor (b/b Hotel), visits to Cairo (b/b accommodation) & the Red Sea (full board).

I am assured by my contact in Egypt that the 2 weeks are well worth it for anyone who has the time. In Cairo you can buy all your Xmas gifts plus enough household linen and clothes for prices that are amazing.

Travel is via

Dublin - Paris (Ryanair/Beauvais) - (Charles De Gaulle) - Luxor - Paris (Charles de Gaulle) - (Ryanair/Beauvais) for those only coming for 1 week and

Dublin - Paris (as above) - Luxor -Hurgada -Paris (as above) for those doing the 2 weeks.

Family members/friends can also come along. There are plenty of organised group trips and activities for them to take part in while you are studying. The costs to them would be the total less the course fee.

Cost

The 1 week trip is 1150.00 (approx).

The 2 week trip is 1480.00 (approx) - deduct 350.00 in both cases if not attending the course.

The Foundation course will be taught on the cruiser and other venues as mentioned. The Intermediate will be taught in Luxor, Cairo & Red Sea Hotels.

Payment

If you are interested, or know anyone who is, please phone/email me. You will need to forward a deposit of 150.00eu immediately for the trip (non-refundable) and a 50.00eu deposit for the course.

Please understand that the Dublin - Paris - Dublin section of the trip is by Ryanair so there are no refunds from them under any circumstances. These are usually booked by individuals but the contact in Egypt, has offered to book and pay the Paris leg of the trip, if I have received the deposits.The reason for this is that Ryanair reduce their prices suddenly and we want to take advantage of this whenever that happens. Regardless of what happens, this portion cannot be refunded as Ryanair don't do refunds no matter what the circumstances. For the same reason if, for any reason, you miss the connecting flights we cannot provide you with an alternative flight or a refund. This is unlikely to happen as there has been plenty of time allowed between connecting flights.

Please note also that Ryanair do not transfer luggage on so you have to take care of this yourself. Transport from one airport to another within Paris involves 2 buses at 10 eu approx per bus which you pay at the time.

The following details are neccessary to make the Ryanair bookings:

· Name, as it is on your passport (Ryanair are very strict about this - if there is any query they simply will not let you on a flight). · An email address to get confirmation that the flight has been booked containing details of valid number for getting on the flight (Ryanair is a tickless airline). (If you don't have one, we will arrange something for you).· A contact phone number. · Your address.

Note:

A couple of additional points: You need to take out your own insurance.You might want to check out any immunisations you need. You need a passport that is valid for 6 months after your trip to Egypt (ie from November 2003). Visas are collectively applied for on arrival.

Week 2 Itinerary (2 nights LUxor, 2 nights Cairo, 3 nights Red Sea) Return Hurgada - Paris -Dublin as above.

* 6 nights nile cruise - 5 Star Cruise Ship - Full Board: All meals are open buffet, wide selection, high quality. Fully Air Conditioned throughout - the Cabins are en-suite and amenities include Sun Deck, Swimming Pool (towels provided) Bar & Lounge, Same day laundry service available.
