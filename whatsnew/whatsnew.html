This part of the site is intended to support a living, sharing community of subscribers. We have therefore created a discussion group based on the Yahoo! Groups model. To participate in this forum, subscribers should enter their e-mail address in the form below and click the Join Now button. It will then be added to the list of members for the group. Members e-mail each other, discuss items, relate stories of their experiences and otherwise keep in touch.

It is also worthwhile for you to learn how to use the Yahoo Groups site and register an ID with the site. You can then access areas of the group's site such as the Files section, which allows members enter into a storage area, upload different files for storage (eg the notes from a workshop) and notify others of their upload.
