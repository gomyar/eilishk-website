AURA-SOMA TRAINING

LEVELS 1 - 4

These courses are certified by ASIACT in the U.K, which is an educational trust dedicated to colour and research in colour. ASIACT has its own training premises in Lincolnshire, runs a worldwide network of training services to teachers and practitioners of Aura-Soma and contributes to international seminars on colour. It also has both an extensive in-house programme of courses related to Aura-Soma and conducts a similar visiting programme internationally. There is an annual congress (usually in some place like Thailand or Mexico) and an annual trip to places of interest (such as Hawaii or Peru etc). It has a training website (www.asiact.org) and a product website (www.aura-soma.net), a practitioner newsletter and quarterly magazine. Aura-Soma Ireland has a national distributor, a network of practitioners & teachers, a practitioner newsletter, a website (www.aura-soma-ireland.com), regular training and colour courses and practice opportunities.

Becoming a Practitioner

You may commence practising, in a limited way, at any point after you have completed the first two levels of the Practitioner Training* provided you agree to observe the standards and practices set out in the Aura-Soma Practitioner Guide. Registered Practitioners receive a Practising Certificate and are listed on ASIACT's Practitioner Register. To keep Practitioners up to date with the Aura-Soma Colour System the Renewal Process has been created. The Renewal Process nurtures the development of Practitioner skills and may be seen as a continuing professional development programme (CPD). *Note further training requirements to become a Practitioner are being introduced on a country by country basis. Please contact the Academy for specific details.

Level 1, The Foundation Course

is a stand alone course and is useful:

* For anyone interested in getting a basic introduction to colour

* For the opportunity to relate colour and Aura-Soma to your current practice, what ever that might be, and see how it might fit in and enhance your practice

* For the opportunity to experience colour directly and track it's effects on your own process ie to have both a rational and intuitive experience of colour and how that affects you as an individual and as part of a group

* For the first part of the 4 level training process for those interested in becoming Aura-Soma practitioners.

The Course Covers in Depth

The origins of Aura-Soma

Colour Theory

Colour and the Chakras

Physical aspects of colour·

Notes on the 104 bottles

Interpreting the colour selections

Opportunities to practice interpreting the colour selections under supervision

Meaning and use of Pomanders, Quintessences and Essences

Colour sets in Aura-Soma (chakra, rescue, angel etc)

The Masters and Aura-Soma

Cos-medics/Nutri-ceuticals

Practical uses for colour in your work can be examined (eg bio-energy, reflexology, reiki, tarot, psychics etc)

Follow up practice days will be available every few months after the course is finished.

Further Training in Aura-Soma

The training is in 4 parts, levels 1 - 4. For those wishing to continue with the training after the Foundation course the next 2 levels introduce colour with increasing complexity. The 4th level consists of a piece of original written work/research, a minimum 36 hour attendance at either a recognized anatomy/physiology course or a counselling course (unless this has been previously acquired elsewhere) and 60 hours of professionally recorded consultations.
