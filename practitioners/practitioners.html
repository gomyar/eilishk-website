Aura-Soma Practitioner training currently comprises 4 levels. Students may become Practitioners at any time after the completion of Level 2 but since they are still near to the beginning of the training, it is suggested that they develop their practice with the public in a gradual way.

At Level 3 the Practitioner may work increasingly with the public and at Level 4 Practitioners have demonstrated experience of working with Aura-Soma and understanding of the principles of Aura-Soma and may develop a full public practice. The level of training attained by the Practitioner is featured on their Practising Certificate, which is valid for 2 years.

This page features a list by county and level of Aura-Soma practitioners in Ireland, along with their full contact details and a brief list of their skills and areas of speciality. Simply click the relevant county name and you will see a list of everyone available to work in that county.
