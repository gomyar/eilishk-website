Aura-Soma Colour Therapy

"You are the colours you choose"

Aura-Soma is a non-intrusive colour system of great beauty which offers the opportunity for awareness and transformation. It invites you to work from the deepest level of your being, empowering you to help yourself. Through colour, Aura-Soma brings consciousness and insight from a deep inner level to your everyday life. Aura-Soma is especially suited for those who wish to come to a new focus in their life and/or who wish to develop themselves.

Aura-Soma is not a therapy in the traditional sense since it makes no claims to heal medical conditions. What it does do is address the whole question of what lies behind dis-ease. Aura-Soma is about what is right with you, not what is wrong with you. It supports you to come to a greater ease within yourself.

Your choice of dual coloured bottles, called Equilibrium, is the key to an individual Aura-Soma Consultation. You will be invited to choose that which you are most drawn to, from more than 100 bottles. During the consultation that follows you will gain insight into your current situation and the larger pattern of your life story. Your colour choice reveals your gifts, challenges and opportunities. What do you need to overcome so that your gifts can come through? It is not fortune telling. Your colour choice will also shed light on your potential for growth and transformation.
